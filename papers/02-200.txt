Date:    15 May 02
Subject: 754r Liaison Charge

At 161, J3 appointed Larry Meadows to be our liaison to 754R
Working Group of the IEEE/CS Microprocessor
Standards Committee (754R).  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by 754R which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which 754R requests that the liaison report to J3; and

To report to 754R the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by 754R to which 754R requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to 754R.
