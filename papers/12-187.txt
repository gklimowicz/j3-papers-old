Subject: MPI Liaison Charge

At 199, J3 appointed Bill Long to be our liaison to the MPI Forum
Fortran Working Group.  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by MPI which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which MPI requests that the liaison report to J3; and

To report to MPI the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by MPI to which MPI requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to MPI.
