1. Introduction

A subprogram is the text that defines a procedure.  There is only one
instance of it.  Subclause 12.6.2.4 should be entitled "Instances of a
procedure".  The body of the subclause is schizophrenic and frequently in
its usage of the terms "subprogram" and "procedure".
