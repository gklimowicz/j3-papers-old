From:    Dan Nagle

Date:    03 May 2004
Subject: J11 Liaison Charge

At J3 meeting 168, J3 appointed Craig Rasmussen to be our liaison to J11
(the C programming language) International Standards Committee.

The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by J11 which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which J11 requests that the liaison report to J3;

and,

To report to J11 the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by J11 to which J11 requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to J11.
