Date:    2012 October 16
Subject: UPC Liaison Charge

At 199, J3 appointed Damian Rouson to be our liaison to the
Unified Parallel C Consortium.  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by UPC which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which UPC requests that the liaison report to J3; and

To report to UPC the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by UPC to which UPC requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to UPC.
