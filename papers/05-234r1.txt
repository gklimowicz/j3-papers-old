Subject: Specifications, Syntax, and Edits for rank = 15
References: Feature j3-039, 05-144r2
--------------------------------------------------------------

Summary:
--------

Relax the restriction on the maximum rank of an array.

Specification:
--------------

Allow the sum of the rank and co-rank of an object to be
up to fifteen.
