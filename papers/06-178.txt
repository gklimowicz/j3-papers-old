
                                                          J3/06-178

Date:    08 May 2006
From:    Dan Nagle
To:      J3
Subject: OWG Liaison Charge

At meeting 176, J3 appointed Dan Nagle to be our liaison
to ISO/IEC Project 22.24772 (OWG).  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by OWG which may
affect the Fortran standard, or which OWG requests that the liaison report
to J3; and

To report to OWG the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by OWG to which OWG requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to OWG.
