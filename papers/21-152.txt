The IEEE Standards Association issued a Call for
Participation for an IEEE 754 study group.  The
name of the study group is IEEE Study Group on
Additional Floating-Point Formats to Support
Machine Learning.The study group meets virtually
from 12:00 Noon - 1:00 PM ET (US) on the fourth
Monday of each month.  The study group first met
on 2021 April 26.

To join the meetings, contact the IEEE 754 Study
Group Chair Kiran Gunnam at

kirangunnam.ieee@gmail.com

or the IEEE SA Program Manager Tom Thompson at

thomas.thompson@ieee.org

Mike Cowlishaw, the former IEEE 754 editor, has
prepared a a preliminary errata page for
IEEE Std 754-2019.  The URL for the page is

http://speleotrove.com/misc/IEEE754-errata-2019.html
