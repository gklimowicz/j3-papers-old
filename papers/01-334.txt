Date:    22 August 2001
From:    Dan Nagle
To:      J3
Subject: Database Liaison Charge

At meeting 158, J3 appointed Craig Dedo to be our liaison to H2,
the NCITS Database Committee.  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by H2 which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which H2 requests that the liaison report to J3; and

To report to H2 the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by H2 to which H2 requests a reply
from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to H2.
