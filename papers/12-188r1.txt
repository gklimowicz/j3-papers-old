Subject: UPC Liaison Charge

At PL22.3 meeting 199, PL22.3 appointed Damian Rouson to be our liaison
to the Unified Parallel C Consortium.  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to Pl22.3 any questions and/or positions taken by UPC
which may affect the Fortran standard, or which UPC requests
that the liaison report to PL22.3; and

To report to UPC the position expressed by PL22.3 by recorded PL22.3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by UPC to which UPC requests a reply
from PL22.3,  or any issue regarding which PL22.3 by recorded PL22.3 vote
wishes to make its point of view known to UPC.
