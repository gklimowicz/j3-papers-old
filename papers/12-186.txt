Subject: OpenMP Liaison Charge

At 199, J3 appointed Bill Long to be our liaison to the OpenMP
Architecture Review Board.  The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by OpenMP which may affect
the Fortran standard, or which OpenMP requests that the liaison report to J3;
and

To report to OpenMP the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by OpenMP to which OpenMP requests
a reply from J3,  or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded J3 vote wishes
to make its point of view known to OpenMP.
