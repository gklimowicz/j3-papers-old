
                                                               J3/07-300

From:    Van Snyder
To:      J3
Subject: IFIP WG 2.5 Liaison Charge
Date: 2007 October 12

At J3 meeting 182, J3 appointed Van Snyder to be our liaison to the
International Federation of Information Processing Societies Working
Group 2.5 (Mathematical Software).

The charge to the liaison is:

To report to J3 any questions and/or positions taken by IFIP WG 2.5 that
may affect the Fortran standard, or that IFIP WG 2.5 requests that the
liaison report to J3;

and,

To report to IFIP WG 2.5 the position expressed by J3 by recorded J3 vote
on any questions made or issues raised by IFIP WG 2.5 to which IFIP WG
2.5 requests a reply from J3, or any issue regarding which J3 by recorded
J3 vote wishes to make its point of view known to IFIP WG 2.5.
