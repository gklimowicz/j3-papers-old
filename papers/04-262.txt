Title: Triplets in substring subscripts.

Submitted by: J3

Status: For Consideration

References: FCD §6.1.1

Basic Functionality: 
Allow stride in substring subscripts.

Rationale: 
It is occasionally desirable to extract non-contiguous
characters from a string.

Estimated Impact: 
Probably little.  

Detailed Specification: 
Similar to the specification of array triplets.  The
syntax to be extended is R611.
